MQTT Demo

   

Simple Gauge

Step 1 : Fill in your own broker or use the one from the kidn people at broker.mqttdashboard.com. (but be gentle...) 

    var client = new Messaging.Client('broker.mqttdashboard.com', 8000, 'myclientid1234');

Step 2 : Have the javascript client subscribe to a topic. 

    client.subscribe('testGaugeTopic/#', {qos: 2});

Step 3 : Start sending messages by copying this into your shell... 

    for i in {1..100}
    do
        mosquitto_pub -h broker.mqttdashboard.com -t testGaugeTopic/liveData --qos 2 -m "$(((RANDOM%120)+1))"
    done
